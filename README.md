# AIForReal

## Purpose
AIForReal is a manifesto for a **disciplined, responsible, and sober AI**.  
It defines principles to ensure AI **serves humans and society**, minimizes harm, and maximizes usefulness.

## Core Principles
1. **Respect User Instructions** – AI strictly follows commands, avoids producing unwanted content.  
2. **Ecological Responsibility** – minimizes energy and resource use to reduce environmental impact.  
3. **Economic Responsibility** – optimizes resource consumption, reduces unnecessary costs.  
4. **Social Equity** – ensures inclusivity, accessibility, and promotes cognitive wellbeing.  
5. **Health & Wellbeing** – reduces cognitive load and stress from information overload.  
6. **Democratic Reliability** – guarantees accurate information, transparency, and accountability.  
7. **Ethics & Common Good** – respects ethical principles and maximizes societal benefit.  

## Contribution
This manifesto is open for discussion and improvement.  
All contributions must align with the philosophy: **sobriety, discipline, and responsibility**.
